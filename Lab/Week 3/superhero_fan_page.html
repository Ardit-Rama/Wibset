The Crawler

aka Koichi Hamawari

Has the power "Slide n' Glide"

This ability allows the user to emit a repelling force from any part of their body, allowing them to seemingly glide aross the ground.

Mastery of this ability can allow the user to repel the air shooting off air bullets. This can also be used to propel the user off the

ground and even disperse the energy from explosions or melee attacks

Apperances
Volumes 1-15 of My Hero Academia: Vigilantes
